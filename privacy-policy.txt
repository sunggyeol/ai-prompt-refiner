# Privacy Policy for Instant Prompt Optimizer

## Data Collection
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data. 

## API Key Storage
Your Google Gemini API key is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This key never leaves your device except for direct API calls to Google's Gemini service.

## Text Processing
Selected text is sent directly to Google's Gemini API for optimization. We do not store, log, or have access to this text. All processing is handled by Google's servers according to their privacy policy.

## Third-Party Services
This extension uses Google's Gemini API. Your use of this service is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
